Game Instructions

How to Play

Master the game with these simple instructions.

1

Match Cookies

Find and connect matching cookie pairs to remove them from the board.

2

Clear the Board

Plan your moves strategically to clear all cookies before the timer runs out.

3

Level Up

Progress through increasingly challenging levels with new cookie patterns and obstacles.

Remember, cookies can only be matched if they have at least one side free or are on top of the pile. Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting stuck!
